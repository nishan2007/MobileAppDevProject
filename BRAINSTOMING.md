    **TOP 3:**
1.Simple Social Hub (Social Media App)

Mobile: Yes, this is a mobile-first application designed for iOS and Android where users can quickly post, scroll, and interact with content on the go.

Story: The app tells the story of everyday users sharing moments, thoughts, and updates with friends in a simple and distraction-free environment. It focuses on real, quick sharing rather than complex features.

Market: The target audience is students and young adults who want a lightweight social platform without the clutter of larger apps like Instagram or TikTok.

Habit: This is a high-frequency daily use app. Users are expected to open it multiple times a day to check posts, like content, and share updates.

Scope: The scope is moderate. It includes core features like authentication, posting, likes, comments, and a feed, but avoids advanced features like messaging, stories, or live streaming.



2.Event Planner App

Mobile: Yes, the app is designed for mobile devices so users can manage and track events anytime and anywhere.

Story: The app helps users organize their personal and professional events efficiently. It focuses on simplifying planning by keeping all event details, tasks, and attendees in one place.

Market: The target audience includes students, professionals, and small groups who frequently organize events such as meetings, parties, or study sessions.

Habit: This is a medium-frequency app. Users will use it more heavily when planning events, but less frequently when no events are upcoming.

Scope: The scope is moderate to slightly broad. It includes event creation, invitations, RSVP tracking, and task management, but avoids complex integrations like payments or large-scale event hosting systems.



3.Local Deals Finder

Mobile: Yes, this is a mobile app designed for users to quickly find nearby deals based on their location.

Story: The app connects users with local businesses by showcasing real-time deals and discounts. It helps users save money while helping businesses attract more customers.

Market: The target audience includes budget-conscious consumers, students, and local shoppers looking for discounts in their area.

Habit: This is a moderate-frequency app. Users may check it daily or a few times a week, especially when planning purchases or outings.

Scope: The scope is moderate. It includes browsing deals, filtering by category, saving favorites, and basic location services, but avoids complex features like full e-commerce or payment processing.
